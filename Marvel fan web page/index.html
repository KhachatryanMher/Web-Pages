Marvel's New Approach to Content Production

The Marvel cinematic universe returns with series after a long hiatus.The first of five Marvel superhero series planned for this year is "Wanda/Vision" launched on Disney+. But according to Kevin Feige, not all Marvel series will have a second or third season. The studio plans to adhere to a more flexible scheme, when characters from the series move to full-length films, and from there can return to TV screens.
